Our Story

Green Colibri was born from a shared passion for exceptional coffee and sustainable practices. Our name comes from the hummingbird (colibrí), a symbol of joy, energy, and the ability to find sweetness in life.

We source our beans directly from farmers who practice sustainable agriculture, ensuring fair trade and exceptional quality. Every bean is roasted in-house to perfection, unlocking its full flavor potential.

Located in the heart of Nicosia, our cafe is a haven for coffee enthusiasts and those seeking a moment of tranquility amidst the city bustle. Our skilled baristas are dedicated to crafting each cup with precision and care.

Our Dark Roast Espresso

Perfect for those who love a powerful coffee kick. This bold espresso has deep chocolatey notes with a hint of spice. Try it as a straight shot or in a macchiato.

Recommended: Double Espresso or Macchiato

House Blend Pour Over

A beautifully balanced cup with notes of caramel, nuts, and a touch of citrus. Our most popular choice for those who appreciate complexity without bitterness.

Recommended: Pour Over or Americano

Ethiopian Yirgacheffe

Light roasted to highlight its natural berry and floral notes. This single-origin coffee is like a fruit tea with a clean, bright finish.

Recommended: Chemex or V60 Pour Over

Velvet Cappuccino
